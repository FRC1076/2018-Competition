
Driver:
  Left joystick: controls speed/going forward
    - y values
  right joystick: controls turning
    - x values
  triggers: control gear shifting

Operator:
  right joystick: elevator up and down
    - y values

  one trigger: absorb with Grabber
  two triggers: spit with Grabber

  Y button: right wing up
  B button: right wing down
  DPad up or down: left wing up or down
  as a safety: driver controller must be pressing down the same controls
  in order to move wings up or down
