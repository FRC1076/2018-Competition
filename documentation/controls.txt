
Driver:
  Left joystick: controls speed/going forward
    - y values
  right joystick: controls turning
    - x values
  triggers: control gear shifting

Operator:
  hold A and B on both controllers as safety (w wings)
  bumpers (with safety held): unlock wing latches
    - once wings are down, triggers will raise the wings
    - with just operator safety: the bumper lowers the wing
  right joystick: elevator up and down
    - y values
  left joystick:
  one trigger: take with Grabber
  two triggers: spit with grabber
